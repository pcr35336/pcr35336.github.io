Bexley Rogers

Bexley is an English Labrador who was adopted by the Rogers Family on May 25, 2013, to which he has now grown to be a huge good boy. He was 8 weeks old when they first got him and have considered getting him a baby brother Corgi good boy, to which he is very excited about.

He has learned many tricks and is very disciplined. He is very respectful of his toys and all the new friends he meets everyday. He has a minor licking problem but he does it out of love.

Bexley also has a huge appetite for treats and it has led to a weight problem, although he recently was given a "dog fit-bit" that tracks and monitors his walking and calorie burning activities and is on his way to a healthy body again.
